Scott Nichols

Improving Lives. Better Policies.

Lived in Colombia and the United States. Exploring the world for a new home.

I’m a social science researcher specializing in education, sociology, and psychology. I collaborate with schools, companies, governments, families, and individuals to solve complex problems. I rely on empirical and analytical methods in my research, emphasizing qual/quan and case study methods. I adjunct teach university courses in education, psychology, and sociology.

I completed my doctoral work in Education specializing in literacy and social research and my Bachelors and Masters in Educational Psychology specializing in human development. I completed certificates at Columbia University, École normale supérieure, and Harvard University in my fields of research. My dissertation (The Effects of Cultural Capital, Social Capital, and Economic Capital on Reading Achievement, Reading Motivation, and Reading Activity) examined critical theory from the perspective of cultural capital through case study and quantitative methods. My nonprofit work received grants from federal agencies, NGO’s, and foundations.

The capabilities section is a brief description of my services and topics that I cover. I have created some examples that show select studies I may be requested to complete. The countries section provides the most recent data on population, education, GDP and so forth provided by the OECD for a few selected countries.

To setup a meeting send me a message. I’m happy to travel anywhere in the world or work virtually with you or your organization. English is my native language, my Spanish is at level II and my French is at level I.
