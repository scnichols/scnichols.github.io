In my work as a licensed psychologist, I specialize in helping governments, corporations and organizations find perfect employees with the end result being to help both the business and potential candidates or current employees find work that will lead to success for both the business and the individual. The end result is to find the perfect candidate that will be successful long-term in their line of work.

I use an integrative approach in my work. I provide psychological testing and assessments utilizing both qualitative and quantitative methodologies. The organization receives a full-report along with a brief for each candidate along with professional recommendations.

My office is located in Bogotá, Colombia but I am able to assist any organization throughout the world.
